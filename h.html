PROJECTS

Project 1-Business Website

The project aims to establish a comprehensive Business Website dedicated to providing detailed information about the training facilities offered by a Martial Arts center. Our primary objective is to create a user-friendly platform that serves as a gateway for individuals seeking information on Martial Arts programs and facilitates easy access to contact details for enrollment inquiries. The scope of the project encompasses a thorough presentation of the training center's offerings, allowing users to explore various programs and gain valuable insights into the overall services provided. The Business Website is designed to address the need for an accessible and informative platform, offering a solution to showcase training opportunities, while ensuring the security of data managed by a dedicated administrator. This initiative addresses the problem of a lack of such a platform, providing individuals with a valuable resource to explore and engage with the Martial Arts training center.

Project 2-Stent Analysis using Ansys

This project uses ANSYS to simulate and analyze cardiovascular stents during balloon angioplasty, focusing on mechanical reactions and finite element analysis (FEA). The study examines stent deployment mechanics, emphasizing recoil percentage, stress sites, and residual stresses to meet FDA standards. It includes pre-analysis of the mathematical model, addressing contact and material nonlinearities, and highlights the importance of sub-step iterations in high-deflection scenarios. The project provides a step-by-step tutorial for setting up ANSYS simulations, considering material properties and hyperelasticity parameters.
